Our Commitment to Sustainable Waste Recycling


At yeshivat lapid modiin, we recognize the critical role that waste recycling plays in protecting our planet's resources.
As part of our commitment to sustainable practices, we have implemented a comprehensive waste recycling
program within our school community. We strive to reduce our environmental impact and promote a cleaner,
healthier future for all. We encourage everyone to join us in our efforts to make a positive
impact on our planet and create a better world for future generations.
